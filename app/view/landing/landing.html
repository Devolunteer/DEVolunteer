Who We Are

We are a team of passionate developers who want to make the world a better place. We come from several different backgrounds but all have one common goal: improving the lives of others through our code. Everything we do is focused on leaving this world better than it was when we found it.

Who You Are

You are one of two people. You are either a member of a Non Profit Organization whose sole focus is helping others, those in need, those who can't speak for themselves. Or, you are a developer who is looking to expand your skills, your portfolio and make a positive impact on the world while doing so.

Why Us?

We combine both worlds, the charitable selflessness of a Non Profit and the keen eye and desire to solve problems of a developer. DEVolunteer allows developers to advertise their skills and availability while providing Non Profit Organizations a single location to find developers to help them with their tech/website needs.
